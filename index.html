Innovation Week 2025

Designing the Future: Innovation Meets AI

Innovation Week 2025 is centered on Innovation with AI, exploring how AI intersects with business and design thinking. Each day highlights a new theme, from sustainability and technology to consulting and entrepreneurship, through keynotes, panels, and hands-on workshops that show how AI and business frameworks can be applied to real-world challenges.
